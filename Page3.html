My Journey & Vision

I’m going to develop my PI, my vision and my competencies by following electives focused around entrepreneurship in B3.1. This will help me reach my vision.

I’m improving my general design abilities by working as the sole industrial designer at the company Zeliox. I work directly with the owner to work out new concepts and improve existing products. I have complete freedom and access to industrial machines to produce my prototypes. I’m learning what it takes to create a real product, how to talk to clients, how to work out and present my concepts, how to sketch professionally, how to brainstorm, and much more. I can’t show my work at the company since it’s confidential.

I’m also starting my own company, I’m selling my own 3D products on the platform Etsy and I’m developing several of my own concept into full products and apps. An example is my Fina Line of self-help and self-manegement apps. You can find the prototypes in the end at the projects Folder

To sell these products I need websites – that’s why I’m challenging my coding skills with this portfolio.

By doing these things I push myself to learn.
