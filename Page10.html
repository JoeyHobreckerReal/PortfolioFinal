SMART Goal Reflection

I used a lot of SMART goals in the last two years to help me work on my personal and professional Identity. They are useful tools but don't guarantee growth, but they help put the focus on critical areas.

Takeaway

I use SMART goals to nudge me in the correct direction – they are not a strict system. I growth the most when I just stayed focused, present and active in my daily activities. I will keep setting small goals, but I will base my growth on my intrinsic motivation and daily activities.
