My Vision

Reconnecting humanity with technology

I will create technology and interactions that will restore a balance between human nature and the digital world – I will create products and systems that are organic and harmonious with our beings. AI technology will be at the core of this.

It's a response to the current social trends like smartphone addictions and echo chamber algorithms. It is time for mindful technology. I envision platforms that connect without addicting and games that push children to go play outside.

It is also a personal fight for me – I'm, like many others, addicted to my phone and the internet. I check it compulsively, search with it for happiness without finding any and bury myself in computer games to distract myself from real life issues.

My vision is for myself – I will help myself to become free.

I worked towards it with projects like KitchenCompass, where I created a new integration and interaction based on AI technology. Kitchencompass was my project for CBL3 for the Artifice Squad. We created an AI cooking assistant that generated recipes based on the ingredients you had at home.

Another course helped me shape my vision – More than Human design. I learned how to look differently at nature and how to integrate it as a design partner.

It is rooted in who I am as a designer – creative, adaptable and a bit lazy. I search for the most simple and creative solutions.

To reach my goals I will follow electives in B3.1 based around entrepreneurship. I don't have the knowledge to make the changes I dream of – but I can bring together people who have it. I want to start a company based on creating these natural interactions with technology.

I already put this dream into motion – I'm filing a request to start my own design firm called HBRK-Design at the Kamer van Koophandel (KvK).
